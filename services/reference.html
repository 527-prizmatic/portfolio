Description

A reference commission is a collaborative process to build a complete reference sheet for a character. No matter how well-fleshed-out your concept is at the moment, I also offer my help with polishing your drafts and rough ideas into a finished product.

Such a commission may include as many shots as you wish. The rendering is usually a little less advanced than in actual character art commissions, and those shots can only be simple or bind poses.
Roughhewn sketches may be requested for more advanced reference, such as showing a character in a specific action/dynamic pose.

Pricing

All prices are given in USD.

A reference sheet commission always comes with one full-body, fully-rendered view, setting a base price of $60. You may request for more (pricing varies depending on differences with the initial one).

Pricing may heavily vary depending on many factors (complexity of the character, how fleshed-out it is, etc.). A finished product may be between $100 and $300.

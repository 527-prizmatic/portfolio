Description

An illustration commission is a complex scene involving multiple characters and some kind of action going on, most likely in a specific environment. Those are the way to go if you want a campfire evening or an epic samurai battle!

Such a commission may include up to eight characters in any pose or style you'd like, and you may request for multiple variations to showcase different outfits or backgrounds.

Pricing

All prices are given in USD for a single character, cumulative for multiple characters.

Base price per character: $60
More complex character designs increase base price by 25% (to $75)
Outfit/design variations: $10 (recolors), $30 (whole new design) each

One item/prop is included in the base price; extra props cost an extra $10 each

Backgrounds: $20 each (minimum of one). Note that this is a base price and may increase depending on complexity (a nightly cityscape is a bit more complex than a high school corridor).

Description

An emote commission includes a pack of highly stylized character portraits for use as emoji in your Discord server (or really anything that accepts custom emoji).

Such a commission may include as many emotes as you want of as many characters as you want, only as portraits (no full-body SD).

Pricing

All prices are given in USD.

Base price per emote: $15 (first of a set), $10 (subsequent)
A "set" designates all emotes representing one given character.
Price is doubled for animated emotes.

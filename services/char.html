Description

A character art commission consists in a fully-rendered, simple depiction of one or multiple characters. Such a piece is relatively basic, showing the character(s) in a static or bind pose.

Such a commission may include up to six for half-body shots or up to three for full-body shots. For each character, one item/prop is included in the price; more can be added for an extra.
Please refer to icon/token commissions for portraits.

Pricing

All prices are given in USD for a single character, cumulative for multiple characters.

Base price per character: $40 (half-body), $60 (full-body)
More complex character designs increase base price by 25% (to $50/75)

One item/prop is included in the base price; extra props cost an extra $10 each
Simple backgrounds only (transparent triangle, solid color, or abstract pattern, all free)

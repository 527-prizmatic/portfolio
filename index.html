Meet the Artist!

Prizmagistus (or Prizma for shorts) is a self-taught French artist with more than five years of experience in the field of illustration and character art, as well as a musician, conlanger, worldbuilder and game development student.

I initially began my creative journey with video game fan arts (most notably Minecraft and Undertale), and quickly developed my own cast of original characters to practice various forms of art. Now I am putting my expertise to your service to bring your own creations to life!

By exploring my Gallery you can get a first idea of my work method as well as my style. I work a lot with contrasting colors, sharp geometrical shapes and thick, powerful lines, all while keeping a largely realistic approach to shapes and anatomy, striving for a somewhat techy, futuristic aesthetic. Of course this does not mean I limit myself to futuristic works - I dabble a lot in fantasy and retro-futurism as well.

I love experimenting with new techniques and trying to integrate them in my tool set, and hope to get to explore entirely new domains through commissions!
